Seattle Summer Staycations

Seattle summers can get really hot and uncomfortable, especially because the typical Seattle home does not come with air conditioning. Sometimes when you just don't have time for a full weekend getaway, you need a quick one-day escape within the city. Check out our suggestions for you below!
